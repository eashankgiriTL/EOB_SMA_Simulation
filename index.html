Slime Mould Algorithm (SMA) — quick notes

Idea. Agents mimic slime mould foraging: sense food, expand, compete/cooperate; veins thicken around good routes → near‑optimal networks.

Phases

  • Approach food (explore): randomised steps guided by fitness gradients.
  • Wrap food (exploit): contract/thicken around promising paths.

Core update (concept)

Blend best‑seen position and two random peers; or contract locally based on p, with oscillating weight W and jitters vb, vc. This maintains exploration while biasing to good routes.

Complexity

≈ O(D + T·N·(1 + logN + D)).
